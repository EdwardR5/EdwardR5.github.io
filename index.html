A New Branch

S Plus Music is a brand new branch of S Plus. The second part of S Plus Entertainment, but the first new department. Created by Edward, the man behind S Plus Entertainment, S Plus Music incorporates members from S Plus Gaming with new members, who want to share their musical talent.

The members of S Plus Music are free to share music they've created and songs they've covered on YouTube.
