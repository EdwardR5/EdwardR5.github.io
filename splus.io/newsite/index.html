Introduction

S+ was established in December 2015 based on principles of social enterprise providing consultancy services in Hong Kong. Our goal is to provide bespoke design and product solutions to seniors for residential retrofits in the local community.

In 2016 S+ made its financial and profit targets within the first 6 months of operation and continues on it’s planned trajectory today. The company primarily provides consulting, project and facility management services to selective clients and works by managing channels for both sourcing and service delivery.

Phased throughout 2017, services will include growth of “retail bundle” focused solutions bringing products and services to individual property owners. 2017 is also a year extending community service awareness with continued S+ consultancy and new mentoring programs.

Our Mission

Serve the Hong Kong 60-80 age group by providing safety and sustainability in the home - their own home.

What We Do

The Hong Kong senior community is underserved with regard to housing options that are safe while yielding both the independence and self-respect of an active lifestyle easily within reach of the citizens within the cosmopolitan city. In fact, this goal is supported by both the 2030 Planning Vision for Hong Kong as well as the United Nations Global Goals.

At S+, we put people first in our service with an approach differing from normal contracting. S+ provide professional consultation to understand senior residential occupiers special needs, provide a range of safety products accommodating those needs and then include installation service delivering a customized outcome. S+ specific aim is to cater for everyone’s differing and uniquely personal requirements so they can comfortably enjoy staying in a familiar home environment for Aging-in-place and Independent Living.
